About NutriBot

NutriBot is a nutritional-based application chatbot that offers nutritional advice for your special needs child's diet. It also recommends meals of high nutritional values as well as tacks your calorie intake. You can ask it questions like:

  • Which foods to avoid when you have autism spectrum disorder?
  • Can one take vitamins and supplements if autistic?
  • Please recommend me gluten free recipes

What We Do

Below are the functions of what the bot can do

Track your calories

NutriBot ensures the food you consume doesn't go above your calorie level

Offer nutritional advice

It offers advice to questions associated with your condition

Recommends meals

NutriBot recommends meals that are low in gluten, sugar etc
